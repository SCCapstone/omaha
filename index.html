Project Omaha

Project Omaha is one of Dr. Jose Vidal's student-led Capstone groups from the College of Engineering and Computing of the University of South Carolina working under auspices of Dr. Duncan Buell to investigate Electronic Systems & Software's iVotronic voting machine as used within the State of South Carolina for all election procedures.
